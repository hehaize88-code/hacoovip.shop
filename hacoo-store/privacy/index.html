Privacy policy.

This inspection version of Hacoo Store Guide does not provide account registration, checkout, comments, or contact-form submission. It does not intentionally collect names, postal addresses, payment details, or order data.

Basic technical data

When the site is hosted, the hosting provider may process routine request information such as IP address, browser type, requested page, timestamps, and security signals to deliver and protect the site. The final production analytics configuration will be documented here before launch.

External links

Buttons can open official resources or third-party destinations. Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies. Review the destination before providing personal information.

Cookies

This inspection build does not set advertising or personalization cookies. If analytics or another optional service is introduced before launch, the policy and consent behavior must be updated first.

Children

This guide is not directed to children and does not knowingly request personal information from them.

Changes

The “last updated” date will change when this policy is materially revised. The production site should include an operational contact method before public launch.
