Privacy policy.

Hacoo Store Guide does not provide account registration, checkout, comments, or order-form submission. It does not intentionally collect names, postal addresses, payment details, or order data.

Basic technical data

The hosting provider may process routine request information such as IP address, browser type, requested page, timestamps, and security signals to deliver and protect the site.

External links

Buttons can open official resources or third-party destinations. Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies. Review the destination before providing personal information.

Cookies

The site does not set advertising or personalization cookies. The selected website language may be stored locally in the visitor's browser. If analytics or another optional service is introduced, this policy and any required consent behavior will be updated first.

Children

This guide is not directed to children and does not knowingly request personal information from them.

Changes and questions

The “last updated” date changes when this policy is materially revised. Editorial corrections can be submitted through the public channel listed on the About page. Do not submit personal or order information through that channel.
