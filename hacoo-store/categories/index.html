Before you continue

Three checks for every product page.

A category route speeds up discovery, but the live destination controls availability, checkout, shipping, returns, and support.

01

Match the live variant

Confirm the selected size, color, quantity, product photos, and current price all describe the item you intend to buy.

02

Read the destination terms

Check who takes payment, which delivery estimate applies, and what happens if the item arrives late or does not match.

03

Save the details

Keep a copy of the product page, selected variant, checkout amount, order number, and tracking information.
