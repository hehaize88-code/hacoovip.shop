Independent answers

Hacoo questions, answered without invented promises.

These answers distinguish official Hacoo information from independent product routes. When a policy can change, the official source is linked.

Is hacoo.store the official Hacoo website?

No. hacoo.store is an independent informational guide. It is not affiliated with, operated by, endorsed by, or officially connected to Hacoo.

How do I place an order on Hacoo?

Hacoo's official help page gives four steps: open the Hacoo app, log in, select items and add them to the shopping cart, then check out and pay.

Can I paste a Taobao, Weidian, or 1688 URL into Hacoo?

The official order-help page we reviewed describes choosing products in the Hacoo app. It does not document Hacoo as an agent-style URL submission and warehouse service. Check current official support before relying on a different workflow.

What is a Hacoo spreadsheet?

It is an informal search phrase for organized product links, categories, or finds. It is not automatically official Hacoo content. Read our full explanation to understand the source checks that matter.

Does hacoo.store sell products?

No. This site provides independent information and product navigation. Check the live destination for product availability, checkout, delivery, returns, and support.

Does Hacoo provide QC photos before shipping?

This guide found no official basis for describing Hacoo as a purchasing agent with a universal warehouse QC-photo process. A catalog photo, seller image, user review, and warehouse inspection image are not the same thing. Verify the image source on each live page.

How long does Hacoo shipping take?

During our July 2026 review, Hacoo's shipping page said receiving time is usually about 15–28 days and processing normally takes 3–5 business days. It also published different regional ranges and said dates are guidelines, not guarantees.

Does Hacoo offer 5–7 day delivery?

The shipping page mentions a fastest shipping time of 5–7 workdays for express shipping. That should not be presented as a universal total delivery promise because processing time, destination, stock, and delays still affect the order.

Is Hacoo shipping free?

Do not assume so. Check the current total and shipping terms shown for the exact order. This site does not publish a universal free-shipping claim.

Which countries does Hacoo ship to?

The reviewed official shipping page says Hacoo could ship worldwide and provides regional estimates. Availability and current terms should still be checked for the actual delivery address.

How should I choose a clothing size?

Compare the live listing's garment measurements with a similar item you already own. Confirm whether the chart uses body or garment dimensions and whether units are centimeters or inches.

How should I choose a shoe size?

Measure both feet, use the larger foot, and compare the live chart's stated foot or internal length. Do not assume one EU, UK, or US conversion works across every seller.

Why do some product links stop working?

External listings can be removed, sold out, redirected, or changed. Category routes on this guide are checked periodically, but each live product must be verified again before purchase.

Who handles returns or after-sales support?

The company or platform that takes the order controls the applicable process. For Hacoo orders, use Hacoo's current in-app or official support. For an external destination, read that destination's terms.

Does hacoo.store sell products?

No. It provides independent information and navigation. Payments do not take place on this guide.

Still deciding where to start?

Read the official-order explanation first, then choose a clearly labelled product route.
