Official order flow

Hacoo describes a standard in-app cart checkout.

  • Open the Hacoo app.
  • Log in to the account.
  • Select products and add them to the cart.
  • Check out and pay in the app.

Official delivery wording

Delivery is presented as a range, not a promise.

  • Typical receiving time shown: about 15–28 days.
  • Order processing is described as 3–5 business days.
  • Regional estimates vary; delays can occur.
  • Express shipping is described separately from total receiving time.

Quick answers

Common Hacoo Store Guide questions.

Short answers to the questions that usually block a visitor before they choose the official app or an independent product route.

Is hacoo.store the official Hacoo website?

No. It is an independent informational guide and is not affiliated with, operated by, or endorsed by Hacoo.

What does “Hacoo spreadsheet” mean?

It is a search phrase commonly used for organized product links or category lists. It is not the name of an official Hacoo ordering method. Read the dedicated explanation before using any external links.

Can I paste Taobao or Weidian links into Hacoo?

Hacoo's official order-help page describes selecting products inside the app, adding them to a cart, and checking out. This guide does not present Hacoo as a purchasing agent that accepts arbitrary marketplace URLs.

Does Hacoo provide warehouse QC photos?

We found no official basis for promising a universal warehouse QC-photo workflow. Check each live in-app product and the current official support information before relying on any inspection feature.

What should I check before opening a product route?

Confirm the live product, selected variant, price, delivery wording, return terms, and the company handling checkout before paying.

How long does Hacoo delivery take?

Hacoo's shipping page currently describes typical receiving time as about 15–28 days, plus more specific regional ranges and a 3–5-business-day processing period. Always check the live official page because estimates can change.
