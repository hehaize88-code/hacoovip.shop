Editorial method

Independent by design.

Hacoo Store Guide exists to make Hacoo-related searches clearer. It does not sell products, process payments, operate the Hacoo app, or claim an official relationship with Hacoo.

How information is checked

Official Hacoo facts are checked against pages on hacoo.app or act.hacoo.app and linked directly. Product-discovery pages are reviewed separately, and readers are encouraged to verify live product details before purchase.

How information is reviewed

  • Open official pages before publishing specific order or shipping claims.
  • Record the editorial review date on factual guides.
  • Avoid fixed guarantees when the official source publishes a range.
  • Check external category routes for valid responses.
  • Correct or remove claims that cannot be supported by a live source.

What this guide will not do

It will not invent warehouse QC services, universal free shipping, guaranteed dispatch times, coupon values, seller reliability, or official partnerships. It will not present third-party catalog images as proof of product quality.

Corrections

Policies and pages can change after an editorial review. Readers should treat the official or final transaction destination as authoritative. Contact details can be added after the hacoo.store domain is connected; until then, use the support channel provided by the relevant destination for order-specific questions.
