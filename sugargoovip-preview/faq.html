Frequently Asked Questions

Open each question to read the full answer. The answers describe general shopping-agent workflows; current platform rules should always be verified directly.

Is this the official Sugargoo website?
No. This is an independent guide and product-discovery website.
Does this website sell products?
No. Product cards lead to the linked main shopping site.
How do I place an order?
Open a product link and follow the destination site’s order instructions.
Why should I check QC photos?
QC photos help identify visible problems before international shipping.
How long does shipping take?
Delivery depends on the route, destination and customs.
Can I return an item?
Returns depend on the seller, policy and deadline.
What is chargeable weight?
It is the actual or volumetric weight used for billing.
What should I do before paying for shipping?
Check the item list, QC, address, declaration, packaging, route, insurance and estimated weight.
