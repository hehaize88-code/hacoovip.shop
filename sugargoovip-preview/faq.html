Frequently Asked Questions

Open each question to read the full answer. The answers describe general shopping-agent workflows; current platform rules should always be verified directly.

Is this the official Sugargoo website?
No. This is an independent guide and product-discovery website. It does not claim to be an official Sugargoo property.
Does this website sell products?
No. Product cards lead to the linked main shopping site. This guide site does not process orders or payments.
How do I place an order?
Open a product link, review the product information on the destination site, choose the required variation and follow that site’s checkout or agent-order instructions.
Why should I check QC photos?
QC photos show the item received by the warehouse and help you identify obvious variation, size, color or condition problems before international shipping.
How long does shipping take?
Delivery depends on the route, destination, customs, parcel type and seasonal congestion. Use the current route estimate as a range rather than a guaranteed date.
Can I return an item?
Returns depend on the seller, platform policy and after-sales deadline. Request a return while the item is still in the warehouse whenever possible.
What is chargeable weight?
Chargeable weight is the weight used for billing. It can be the parcel’s actual weight or its volumetric weight, depending on the route.
What should I do before paying for shipping?
Confirm the item list, QC results, address, declaration, packaging request, selected route, insurance option and estimated chargeable weight.
