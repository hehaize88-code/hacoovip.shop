01

FindQC: official facts and practical limits

01What is FindQC, and does it sell or ship products?+

FindQC's current Terms describe it as a QC discovery and aggregation platform. Its services may include link and image search, regular and premium QC, a link converter, size assistant and Discord bot. FindQC does not provide purchasing or forwarding; third parties handle payments, shipping and returns.

02Where do FindQC QC photos, weight and measurements come from?+

FindQC says QC photos, weight, dimensions and defect records generally come from third-party agents. It organizes the material but does not guarantee its accuracy, completeness or authenticity. Treat a public record as research and compare it with the warehouse evidence for your own order.

03Should I use link, image or keyword search?+

FindQC Academy recommends link search when you already have a valid source URL, image search when the item is visually identifiable but its name is unknown, and keyword search for broader exploration. Image quality, angle and wording can affect results, so confirm the marketplace, seller and variant after every match.

04What do Trending, Karma, Reviews and Hauls tell me?+

FindQC describes Trending as discovery based on user behaviour, Karma as community favourites, Review Wall as buyer reviews with QC photos, and Real Hauls as shipped parcels. These are useful discovery signals, not proof that the next unit, seller batch, shipping price or delivery outcome will be the same.

05What happens when a seller removes a product?+

Some FindQC detail pages keep historical QC photos while clearly marking the product as no longer available and offering a Find Similar route. The archive can help you study visible details, but the removed listing cannot be treated as a current purchase offer.

06Do I need a FindQC account?+

Not every public page requires an account, but FindQC's Terms say that certain features, including My Collection and History, require Google OAuth sign-in. Check the current sign-in prompt and privacy information before using account-linked features.

07Can Real Hauls predict my shipping cost or delivery time?+

No. FindQC's analytics says logistics and tracking are handled by MyCNBox and that FindQC receives anonymized aggregate statistics. Country, route, parcel weight, dimensions, service level and date can change your result, so haul data is context rather than an agent quotation or delivery promise.

08Does FindQC certify authenticity or guarantee QC data?+

No. FindQC's Terms say QC content may be incomplete or contain errors and that the service does not provide authenticity certification. Photos can support a visible comparison, but the buyer remains responsible for evaluating the product, source listing and transaction risk.

02

Using FindQC Pro and the mapped catalog

09Does FindQC Pro sell products?+

No. FindQC Pro is an independent product-discovery and QC education website. Product pages and purchase actions open the source catalog on cnfanshp.com, where availability and current details should be confirmed.

10Are the displayed images a guarantee of product quality?+

No. Images are reference material, not a guarantee. Check the listing details and any warehouse or inspection images made available for your own order before approving international shipment.

11What can I search for?+

Search mapped categories and products by keyword or known product ID. Matching results keep their exact source-page links rather than sending every query to a generic catalog page.

12Why should I check QC images before shipping?+

A careful image review can reveal visible issues such as a wrong colour, obvious damage, missing parts or a measurement mismatch while the parcel is still at the warehouse. It cannot verify every material or performance detail.

13Where do product buttons lead?+

Each product button leads to its matching item page on cnfanshp.com. Category buttons lead to the matching category. External destinations open in a new tab.

14Are prices final?+

No. Any displayed price is a reference copied from the source listing at review time. It may change and may not include domestic shipping, service charges, optional services or international delivery.

15Can FindQC Pro verify authenticity?+

No. FindQC Pro does not authenticate products. A visual checklist can help identify visible mismatches, but it cannot establish authenticity, materials or provenance.

16What if a photo is unclear?+

Describe the exact area and angle you need. A focused request such as “close-up of the left heel seam” is usually more useful than generally asking for more pictures.

17Is FindQC Pro connected to similarly named websites?+

No. FindQC Pro is an independent guide and is not affiliated with FindQC.com, FinderQC.com or any shopping-agent brand.
