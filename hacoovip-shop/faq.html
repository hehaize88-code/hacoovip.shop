Hacoo VIP Site FAQ

This FAQ is now focused on site operation, navigation and technical use. The deeper buying-research questions stay inside the SEO articles, so the FAQ page no longer repeats the same article content.

1. How do I start from the homepage?

Use product cards when you want to open a specific product-detail page. Use category cards when you want to browse a broader product collection.

2. Why are product cards and category cards separated?

They serve different user intent. Product cards are for specific item research, while category cards are for browsing a group such as shoes, jackets or T-shirts.

3. How does the language switcher work?

The switcher changes key interface labels on the current page. It does not send visitors to Google Translate, so users do not leave the site while browsing.

4. Why are some article pages fully English?

The SEO article section is currently written in English first because the target long-tail searches are English phrases such as spreadsheet guide, QC guide and reverse image search workflow.

5. How are article cards sorted?

Article cards use a date value. The newest article appears first and older articles move backward, while the visible module keeps a compact four-card layout.

6. Where can I find all current SEO articles?

Open the SEO Articles page. It collects the current content hub, including spreadsheet, QC photo, reverse image search and reverse-shopping guides.

7. Why do some buttons open cnfanshp.com?

Hacoo VIP is an external discovery guide. Product and spreadsheet buttons send traffic to the main site where users can continue product or category research.

8. Does the site have a sitemap?

Yes. The sitemap is available at /sitemap.xml and includes the homepage, guide pages, FAQ page, article hub and the new SEO article URLs.

9. What should I do if a button opens the wrong page?

Stop using that link and check the label. A product label should open a product page, and a category label should open a category page. Broken links should be corrected before promotion.

10. How often should the content be updated?

For SEO growth, update the article hub regularly. New guides should be added with clear dates, internal links and sitemap entries so Google can discover them.
