Privacy Policy

This policy explains the limited data handling associated with visiting ACBuy.ro. The site is primarily a static informational website.

Information stored in your browser

The website may use local browser storage to remember interface preferences such as the selected language. This information remains associated with your browser unless you clear it.

Hosting and technical logs

The site is delivered through Cloudflare Pages. Hosting and security providers may process standard technical information such as IP address, user agent, requested URL, timestamps and security signals to deliver and protect the website. Their handling is governed by their own policies.

External websites

Product, platform and source links lead to independent third parties. Once you leave ACBuy.ro, the destination website controls its own cookies, accounts, payments and personal information. Review its privacy terms before creating an account or submitting data.

No order data

ACBuy.ro does not operate ACBuy accounts and does not receive your platform password, payment details, warehouse address or parcel information.

Policy updates

This policy may be updated when site functionality or service providers change. The current version applies to use of the site after publication.
