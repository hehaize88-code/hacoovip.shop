Editorial Policy

ACBuy.ro aims to publish useful, cautious and verifiable information about shopping-agent workflows. Our guides distinguish documented platform information from general planning advice.

Source standards

We prioritize official platform pages for service descriptions and workflow claims. Where official pages are incomplete, we may use public carrier, customs or government sources. Community reports can illustrate questions or experiences, but are not treated as universal proof.

Changing information

Shipping prices, routes, exchange rates, storage terms, photo services, payment methods and after-sales rules can change. We avoid presenting temporary figures as permanent guarantees and direct readers to the live checkout or account interface for final terms.

No invented guarantees

We do not guarantee delivery times, customs outcomes, product authenticity, product quality, tax treatment or refund eligibility. QC photographs are described as visual inspection material, not proof of composition, authenticity or durability.

Corrections

Material factual errors should be corrected when reliable evidence is available. Readers can submit a correction request through the Contact page. Updates may include revising wording, replacing a broken source, adding context or removing an unsupported statement.

Commercial independence

External links may lead to third-party websites. Their presence does not convert third-party claims into editorial facts. Editorial explanations should remain understandable without requiring a purchase.
