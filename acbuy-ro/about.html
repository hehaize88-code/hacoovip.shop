About ACBuy.ro

ACBuy.ro is an independent product-discovery and educational website for people researching shopping-agent workflows, warehouse inspection, parcel consolidation and international shipping from China.

What we publish

We organize product references and write practical guides about product links, manual orders, QC photographs, warehouse storage, parcel planning, shipping estimates and destination-specific considerations. The site is designed to help readers understand a process before opening an external platform.

Our independence

ACBuy.ro is not operated, endorsed or officially affiliated with ACBuy, AllChinaBuy, Taobao, Tmall, 1688, Weidian or any brand displayed on linked pages. Platform names are used descriptively so readers can identify the services being discussed.

What we do not do

We do not process payments, purchase products, hold inventory, provide warehouse services, ship parcels or make customs decisions. Prices, availability, exchange rates, fees, routes and platform rules can change. Readers must verify current information on the relevant destination website before paying.

How to use this site

Use product listings as discovery references and guides as educational material. Check the live seller listing, selected variant, current ACBuy interface and destination-country rules before making a decision.
